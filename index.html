Crystals Collector Game

How To Play

Crystals hold unknown values and in most cases, we're left to decipher their value ourselves. In this game, you'll be asked to decipher the unknown values of these crystals by adding them together against a random value you'll be given. Click the crytals and see if you can match the random value before guessing too high!
